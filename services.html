Transient Analysis
Surge & Water Hammer Analysis

Rigorous transient hydraulic modelling using Bentley OpenFlows HAMMER — identifying surge risks, sizing protection equipment, and verifying pipeline pressure ratings under worst-case transient scenarios.

Deliverables
  • Transient simulation model (Bentley HAMMER)
  • Maximum and minimum pressure envelopes
  • Column separation and vapour pocket assessment
  • Surge protection sizing — vessels, air valves, control valves
  • Valve closure time recommendations
  • Technical report with findings and recommendations

Steady-State Analysis
Hydraulic Network Modelling

Comprehensive steady-state hydraulic modelling for water transmission and distribution networks — verifying pressure and velocity profiles, pump duty points, and system performance under peak and minimum demand scenarios.

Deliverables
  • Calibrated hydraulic network model
  • Pressure and velocity profiles for all scenarios
  • Pump operating point verification
  • Hydraulic grade line (HGL) plots
  • System curve and pump curve analysis
  • Technical report with design recommendations
